Harry Potter is the central character in J.K. Rowling's popular series, introduced as an orphaned child living with his abusive aunt and uncle, the Dursleys. On his eleventh birthday, Harry discovers he's a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He learns his parents were killed by the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who also tried to kill him, leaving him with a lightning-bolt-shaped scar. Harry's story follows his journey as he grows into a powerful wizard, makes friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and ultimately confronts Voldemort.
